About Us

Who Are We?

We're a group of high school students running a youth community maker space in the Redmond, WA area.

Redmond STEM Center functions as a hub for students to work, learn, and interact with industry mentors and professionals. Watch the video to learn more about us!

Feel free to reach out to us at contact@redmondstemcenter.org with any questions!
