Web Development Camp

July 18th - 22nd from 10AM - 3PM

Located at the Redmond STEM Center

For 5th - 8th graders. During the RSC Web Development Summer Camp, students will learn the basics of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS as well as the principles behind front-end and back-end development. Students will also explore database technologies and apply the skills they learn to hands-on applications. Course curriculum will be supplemented with in-depth talks given by industry professionals.

$200 per student. Contact us for financial aid.

Robotics Camp

August 22nd - 26th from 10AM - 3PM

Located at the Redmond STEM Center

For 6th - 9th graders. During the RSC Robotics Summer Camp, students will dive into the world of robotics by learning the fundamentals of robotic engineering, programming, and sensor integration. They will get hands-on experience building and programming their own robots to complete various tasks and challenges. Industry professionals will provide in-depth insights into the latest advancements in robotics.

$275 per student. Contact us for financial aid.
