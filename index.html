Who Are We?

We're a group of high school students running a youth community maker space in the Redmond, WA area.

Redmond STEM Center functions as a hub for students to work, learn, and interact with industry mentors and professionals. Watch the video to learn more about us!

Click to see more

Our Mission

At Redmond STEM Center, we are building more than just a makerspace, we are building a community that will:

Foster a collaborative and inclusive environment.

By providing access to profesionals, mentors, enriching events, and other like-minded individuals, students will be able to reach their full potential at our space.

Provide the facilities and equipment for teens to work, connect, and learn.

Students will have their STEM educational resources expanded greatly, and be provided with opportunities for high level projects and working with industry professionals.

Create a sustainable organization that will continue growing.

RSC exists to serve the community and propagate science and technology to teens in the Seattle Area for many years to come.

Connect students to respected STEM organizations.

The STEM center welcomes respected STEM organizations such as teams from FIRST Robotics, Vex Robotics, Rocketry Clubs, and various other interdisciplinary programs.
