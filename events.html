Summer Camps


Robotics Camp

Date: August 12th - 16th, 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Location: Redmond STEM Center

For 5th - 8th graders. The Redmond STEM Center is proud to present our annual Robotics Summer Camp. Students will use Arduino to make basic circuits, then get to code sensors, motors, and other electronics. In the final days, students will create and code a motorized buggy. They will craft motorized attachments to compete in a tournament-style battle-bots competition at the end of the week. Students will get to take home their battle-bots and a full Arduino kit to keep working on projects with the skills they learn. Classes will run 1pm - 5pm, Monday to Friday.

$300 per student. Contact us for financial aid.

Rocketry Camp

Date: August 5th - 9th, 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Location: Redmond STEM Center

For 3rd - 6th graders. In our rocketry camp, students will start with basic principles of aerodynamics and learn about the forces that act on rockets. They will then build and launch their own rockets, testing different designs to see how high they can go. Throughout the week, students will also engage in related activities such as designing a lunar lander and simulating a mission to Mars. Classes will run 8am - 12pm, Monday to Friday.

$250 per student. Contact us for financial aid.
