Summer Camps


Robotics Camp

Date: August 12th - 16th, 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Location: Redmond STEM Center

For 5th - 8th graders. The Redmond STEM Center is proud to present our annual Robotics Summer Camp. Students will use Arduino to make basic circuits, then get to code sensors, motors, and other electronics. In the final days, students will create and code a motorized buggy. They will craft motorized attachments to compete in a tournament style battle-bots competition at the end of the week. Students will get to take home their battle-bots and a full Arduino kit to keep working on projects with the skills they learn. Classes will run 1pm - 5pm, Monday to Friday

Contact us for financial aid.

Rocketry Camp

Date: August 5th - 9th, 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Location: Redmond STEM Center

For 5th - 8th graders. The Redmond STEM Center Rocketry Camp is for any student who has an interest in model rocketry. Students will start the week learning the basics of aerodynamics and rocket design. Throughout the week, they will build and launch their own model rockets. By the end of the camp, students will have the knowledge and experience to continue exploring rocketry on their own.

Contact us for financial aid.

Web Development Camp

Date: August 19th - 23rd, 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Location: Redmond STEM Center

For 6th - 9th graders. The Web Development Camp at Redmond STEM Center is designed to introduce students to the world of web development. Over the course of the week, students will learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and use these skills to create their own personal website. This camp is perfect for students who want to start learning how to build websites from scratch.

Contact us for financial aid.

RC Airplane Camp

Date: August 26th - 30th, 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Location: Redmond STEM Center

For 6th - 9th graders. Join the Redmond STEM Center for an exciting RC Airplane Camp! This camp will teach students the basics of aerodynamics, airplane design, and RC (radio-controlled) technology. Throughout the week, students will design, build, and fly their own RC airplanes. This camp is perfect for students who love aviation and want to learn more about how airplanes work.

Contact us for financial aid.
