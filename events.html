Workshops

Check out our upcoming workshops!

3D Printing Workshop

RSC is hosting a 3D printing workshop with Centro Cultural Mexicano! Students will learn how to use 3D modeling software to model their own little creation. They will also learn how to operate 3D printers, from the software, to setting up prints. At the end of the workshop students will take own their own 3D print!

July 5th (11:00 AM - 2:00 PM)

Located at the Centro Cultural Mexicano

Laser Cutting Workshop

RSC is hosting a Laser Cutting Workshop with Centro Cultural Mexicano! Students will learn how to make their own designs and turn them into a reality with a Glowforge Laser cutter. They will learn all the basics of laser cutting and can take home their creations at the end of the workshop!

July 12th (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM)

Located at the Centro Cultural Mexicano

Summer Camps


Rocketry Camp

Date July 24 - July 28, 9 AM - 3 PM

Location Redmond STEM Center

For 5th - 8th graders. RSC is hosting a Rocketry Summer Camp! Students will learn the science behind how rockets fly, from physics concepts to physical properties. Students will work with simulation software and build their very own rocket. At the end of this summer camp, students will launch their rockets at 60 Acres Park!

$600 per student. Contact us for financial aid.

For 5th - 8th graders. RSC is hosting a Robotics Summer Camp! Students will learn how to program and wire components with Arduino microcontrollers. Students will also learn concepts in mechanical engineering and robotics. At the end of this summer camp, students will take home their very own Arduino project!

$600 per student. Contact us for financial aid.
